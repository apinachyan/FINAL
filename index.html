About characters

Grass

This is Grass. It can multiply and can be eaten by GrassEater. It changes his color depends on weather.

GrassEater

This is GrassEater. It can multiply, eat grasses and be eaten by Wild Animal or Human and die if it has no energy.
His vision gets worse when it is winter.

Wild Animal

This is Wild Animal. It can multiply, eat grasses, grassEaters and humans and die if it has no energy.
The speed of multiplication can be changed. It depends on weather.

Human

This is Human. It can multiply, eat grassEaters and die if it has no energy.
The speed of multiplication can be changed. It depends on weather. When it sees Wild Animal around it starts to run.

Airplane

This is Airplane. It flies and explodes some area by throwing Bomb.
When Bomb explodes it kills everything around the place where bomb was thrown.

Rocket

This is Rocket. It flies and explodes when he gets to either Wild Animal or Human or Airplane.
Rocket explodes and kills everything around. When it can't find anything it explodes at his position.
